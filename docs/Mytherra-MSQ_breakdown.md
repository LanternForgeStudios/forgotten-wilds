Prologue Completion Checklist

By the end of the Prologue, the player has:

✅ Become a Lantern Keeper.

✅ Received the Lantern of the First Promise.

✅ Unlocked the Journal of Legends.

✅ Learned the Five Lantern Keeper Principles.

✅ Met every primary NPC in Ash Hallow.

✅ Unlocked all town services.

✅ Restored the Ash Hallow Town Shrine.

✅ Unlocked Fast Travel.

✅ Left Ash Hallow for the first time.

✅ Reached Hunter's Camp.

✅ Begun investigating the disturbances in Ironwood Trail.
